Cornelia

We are an ongoing research project developed at the History of Art Department of the University of Leuven (KU Leuven). The project aims to analyze the interplay between the dynamics of 17th-century Antwerp and Brussels creative communities and industries (zeroing in on painting and tapestry) on the one hand, and iconographic and stylistic developments on the other.
